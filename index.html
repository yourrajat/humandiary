A TRIP TO CHIKMAGALUR

Chikmagalur is a district and city in the state of Karnataka, India.
It's located in the Mullayagiri district, in the southwestern part of the state, at the base of the Western Ghats' Mullayanagiri Peak.
The city is known for its pleasant climate, tropical rainforest, coffee estates, and tall mountains.
